  • DPB Overview

    2019-09-07

    DPB design aims to provide an extensible framework which abstracts the underlying infrastructure diversity. The architecture of a DPB orchestrator is presented in \fref{DPB-OverArch}. DPB follows a multi-layer design approach and consists of three key layers. At the top of the system is the \defn{aggregator} layer, which is responsible for service persistence, allocation of trunk resources, and end-to-end path computation (\secref{aggregator}). The aggregator layer exposes a single northbound interface (NBI) to the service orchestrator for the management of logical networks across datacenters....
